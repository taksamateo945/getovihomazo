Brett Maher Age, Biography

Brett Maher was born on November 21, 1989, in Fremont, Nebraska, United States. When it comes to nationality, he is American, and his ethnicity is American-White. Brett Maher is the son of Brian (father) and Peggy (mother).

He grew up and spent his childhood with his two sisters, Maggie and Lindsey. According to his birthdate, he is a Scorpio. Maher spent his first three years of high school at Centennial Public Schools in Utica, Nebraska, before transferring to Kearney High School, where he played high school football for the Bearcats.

As a senior, he was an honorable-mention Class A all-state selection. As a senior, he won the state championships in the long jump and pole vault, setting a state record in the latter.
