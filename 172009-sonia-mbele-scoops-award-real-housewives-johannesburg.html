At this point, Sonia Mbele should be nicknamed the odd defier, with how she manages to shine each time she is faced with adversity.

Sonia Mbele Films stuns at the National Film and TV Awards

Her production company, Sonia Mbele Films (SMF), scooped the Best Non-Scripted Show award for the Real Housewives of Johannesburg (RHOJ) at the National Film and TV Awards this past weekend.

Sonia had this to say about her win:

“Thank you to the NFTA awards for recognizing the hard work, blood, sweat and tears we’ve put into this franchise. We had to learn from scratch how to film, plan and shoot this show and it wasn’t easy. "This season was especially hard because I was dealing with health issues, but my weekly iron drips and oxygen tank sessions have literally saved my life, that is why it’s extra sweet to be accepting this award on behalf of everyone who rallied behind RHOJ and SMF.”

Sonia Mbele settles debt with SARS

The victory comes after she clarified issues with the tax man after being accused of 42 tax evasion charges with her former business partners for the production of RHOJ under the previous production company Rhosa before parting ways to start SMF.

Her spokesperson had said she had left Rhosa in 2019, unaware that the company was still operational under SARS, automatically linking her to the incurred fines.

Sonia Mbele opens up about life-changing health condition

In other related Sonia Mbele stories on Briefly News, the Blood and Water actress shed light on her missed court appearances amid the SARS scandal, pointing at an autoimmune disease she caught some months ago.

She also revealed that she has been doing much better after taking her medication and making lifestyle changes like quitting alcohol and changing her diet.
