Queens University Student, Jayvon Pryor, Dies in Charlotte, NC

June 2024 · 2 minute read

In a deeply saddening incident, Jayvon Pryor, a student at Queens University of Charlotte, North Carolina, has died. The university community and beyond are mourning the loss of this promising young life.

A Promising Student’s Queens University

Jayvon Pryor was a cherished member of the Queens University community, known for his enthusiasm and commitment to his studies. His untimely death has sent shockwaves through the university and the wider community.

A Community in Mourning

The news of Jayvon’s death has left the university community in a state of grief. Friends, faculty, and fellow students have been sharing their memories of Jayvon, remembering him as a bright, kind-hearted individual with a promising future.

Queens University Paid Tribute

Queens University has expressed deep sorrow over Jayvon’s passing and is providing counseling services to students who may be affected by this tragic event. The university has asked that the privacy of Jayvon’s family be respected during this difficult time.

Queens University Shared Tribute Post on Facebook by Writing:

Queens mourns the loss of Jayvon Pryor, a member of the men’s track and field team who passed away on December 7, 2023. Today, our hearts are heavy with grief as we lift up Jayvon’s family, loved ones, friends and teammates in our thoughts and prayers.

In honor of Jayvon, a candle light vigil is scheduled on campus this evening from 6:30-7:30 p.m.

Our thoughts are with Jayvon’s family, friends, and the Queens University community during this time of profound loss. We hope they find the strength to navigate through this period of grief and sorrow.
