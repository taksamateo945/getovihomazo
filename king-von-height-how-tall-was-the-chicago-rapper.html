King Von Height: How Tall Was the Chicago Rapper?

July 2024 · 4 minute read

King Von was a rising star in the Chicago drill scene, known for his storytelling skills and gritty lyrics.

Many fans have wondered how tall King Von was, especially since he often appeared taller than his peers in photos and videos.

In this article, we will explore King Von’s height, as well as some other facts about his life and career.

How Tall Was King Von?

According to various sources, King Von’s height was 5 feet 9 inches or 175 cm.

However, King Von did not let his height stop him from pursuing his rap dreams and dominating the music scene.

King Von often wore baggy clothes and sneakers that added some inches to his stature.

What Was King Von’s Real Name?

King Von’s real name was Dayvon Daquan Bennett.

He was born on August 9, 1994, in Chicago, Illinois.

Von grew up in the notorious O Block neighborhood, which is known for its high crime rate and gang violence.

He had six half-siblings from his father, Walter E. Bennett, and three siblings from his mother, Taesha.

King Von’s father was incarcerated for most of his life and was killed by an unknown gunman when King Von was 11 years old.

Von later paid tribute to him in several songs, such as “Wayne’s Story” and “Daddy’s Girl”.

King Von also had a close relationship with his mother, who supported him throughout his rap career.

How Did King Von Become Famous?

Von, a rapper from Chicago, gained popularity with his breakout single “Crazy Story” in 2018.

He released two mixtapes, “Grandson, Vol. 1” and “Levon James,” and his debut studio album, “Welcome to O’Block,” all of which achieved chart success and positive reception.

How Did King Von Die?

Von died on November 6, 2020, after being shot multiple times during a confrontation outside the Monaco Hookah Lounge in Atlanta, Georgia.

According to reports, two groups of men got into an argument that escalated into gunfire.

Von was one of three people who died from their injuries.

Three others were wounded and taken to the hospital.

The police said that they were not involved in the shooting of  Von, but they did exchange fire with some of the gunmen who were fleeing the scene.

Two suspects were detained by the police and could face charges.

Von’s death shocked the hip-hop community and sparked an outpouring of grief and tributes from fans and fellow artists.
