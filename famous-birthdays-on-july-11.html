Famous birthdays on July 11

July 2024 · 2 minute read

Alissa Cara

Alessia Caracciolo turns 27 years old. She was born in Brampton, Canada. Alessia is a Canadian singer-songwriter.

Leisha Hailey

Today is Leisha Hailey’s 49th birthday. Hailey is an American musician and actress who was born on July 11, 1971. She was born to American parents in Okinawa, USCAR (now Japan), and grew up in Bellevue, Nebraska. She came out as a lesbian at 17 and headed to New York City to pursue the American Academy of Dramatic Arts. Her most well-known part was Alice Pieszecki in the Showtime Networks series The L Word and  The L Word: Generation Q. 

Kirk Whalum

Today is Kirk Whalum’s 63rd birthday. Whalum is a songwriter and American jazz saxophonist who was born on July 11, 1958. He was born in the city of Memphis in the state of Tennessee. Whalum’s achievements include The Prince of Tides, Boyz n the Hood, The Bodyguard, Grand Canyon, and Cousins (film).
Whalum was named the National Museum of African American Music’s inaugural Jazz Legend recipient on June 20, 2014. In September 2018, he was honored with a Brass Note on Historic Beale Street in Memphis, Tennessee.

Richie Sambora

Today is Richie Sambora’s 62nd birthday. Sambora, an American rock guitarist, singer, songwriter, and producer, was born on July 11, 1959, and spent 30 years as the lead guitarist of the rock band Bon Jovi. He and Jon Bon Jovi, the band’s main vocalist, established the band’s primary songwriting unit. Stranger In This Town was first published in 1991, followed by Undiscovered Soul in 1998, and Aftermath of the Lowdown in September 2012.
In 2018, as a member of Bon Jovi, Sambora was inducted into the Rock and Roll Hall of Fame, and he reunited with his old bandmates for a performance at the event.
That same year, he and Orianthi created the RSO duet.

Gregory Grunberg

Today is Gregory Phillip Grunberg’s 55th birthday. Grunberg is a film and television actor from the United States who was born on July 11, 1966. He is well-known for his roles in films as Eric Weiss on ABC’s Alias and Matt Parkman on NBC’s Heroes. In the Star Wars sequel trilogy, he was known as Temmin “Snap” Wexley, and in A Star Is Born, he was known as Phil.
